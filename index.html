About CivicLens

What is CivicLens?

CivicLens is a citizen-centric grievance tracking platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local governance. It transforms every pothole, broken streetlight, water leak, and sanitation issue into a trackable, resolvable case.

Our platform ensures 100% transparency — every complaint is publicly visible, every status update is logged, and citizens verify the resolution before a case is closed. No fake closures. No hidden reports.

  • Real-time complaint tracking on interactive maps
  • Photo evidence upload with every complaint
  • Citizen-verified resolution system
  • Public accountability dashboard
Process

How It Works

Simple 4-step process from reporting to resolution

01

Pin the Issue

Open the live map, tap on the exact location, and our GPS system tags precise coordinates automatically.

02

Add Evidence

Upload photos directly from your camera or gallery. Visual evidence helps authorities prioritize the issue.

03

Public Tracking

Track your complaint as it moves from Pending to In Progress to Resolved in real-time on the public dashboard.

04

Verify and Resolve

You personally verify the fix before the report closes — ensuring genuine accountability, no fake closures.
